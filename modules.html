AI FOR ALL
Module 1: Introduction

"AI For All" is a widespread concept and movement focused on democratizing artificial intelligence, aiming to make its benefits, tools, and understanding accessible to every individual and community, regardless of technical background, income, or location.

The core idea emphasizes broad inclusivity, ensuring that AI technologies are not limited to a select few or specific industries. This movement advocates for:

Accessibility: Making AI tools and platforms easy to use and available to a general audience.

Education: Providing resources and training to help people understand how AI works and how it can be used ethically and effectively.

Equity: Striving to ensure that the development and deployment of AI do not reinforce existing biases or create new inequalities.

Ultimately, "AI For All" seeks to empower a diverse population to participate in and benefit from the ongoing AI revolution.
