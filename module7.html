Module 7: Artificial Intelligence in Mathematics

7.1 Introduction

Mathematics forms the foundation of science and technology. AI enhances mathematics by assisting in solving complex problems, discovering patterns, and verifying proofs.

7.2 AI in Mathematics

AI in mathematics uses machine learning, deep learning, symbolic AI, and reinforcement learning to perform reasoning and pattern discovery.

7.3 Role of AI

AI supports automated theorem proving, optimization, numerical analysis, and conjecture generation.

7.4 Scope

AI contributes to pure mathematics, applied mathematics, computational mathematics, statistics, and cryptography.

7.6 Challenges

Challenges include interpretability, data dependency, computational cost, and verification of AI-generated results.

7.7 Future Prospects

AI is expected to help solve open mathematical problems and enhance interdisciplinary research.

7.8 Conclusion

AI acts as a powerful collaborator in mathematics, expanding human capability while preserving logical rigor.
