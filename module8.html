Module 8: Artificial Intelligence in Computer Science

8.1 Introduction

Artificial Intelligence is one of the most influential subfields of Computer Science. It enables machines to perform tasks that require human intelligence such as learning, reasoning, perception, and decision-making.

8.2 Role of AI in Computer Science

AI enhances algorithm design, data processing, intelligent software systems, automation, and decision support systems. It powers recommendation engines, virtual assistants, and optimization systems.

8.3 Scope of AI

AI is applied in software engineering, operating systems, databases, computer networks, cybersecurity, and human-computer interaction. It improves system efficiency, automation, and intelligent decision-making.

8.4 Challenges

AI faces challenges such as computational complexity, data dependency, interpretability issues, security risks, and ethical concerns including bias and privacy.

8.5 Future Prospects

The future of AI in computer science includes explainable AI, intelligent system design, autonomous computing, and next-generation architectures.
